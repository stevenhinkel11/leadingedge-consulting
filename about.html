What Makes Us Different

Going beyond surveys to create meaningful change

While many cultural assessments rely on surveys and questionnaires, we take a more immersive approach. Our methodology involves direct engagement with your team, creating opportunities for authentic reflection and discovery. We believe that true cultural transformation happens when people are actively involved in the process, not just responding to checkboxes.

Our Philosophy & Approach

At Leading Edge Consulting, we believe that organizations and their people thrive when they have a clear understanding of who they are and how they operate. Our mission is to illuminate the often-unseen cultural dynamics that influence performance, relationships, and outcomes.

Every organization has cultural blind spots—subtle patterns that, if left unexamined, can hinder progress and collaboration. We specialize in identifying these patterns using adapted methods from cultural anthropology, then guiding teams through a practical process to bridge the gap between their current and desired culture.

Our work is grounded in empathy, evidence, and action—because clarity isn't just informative, it's transformative.

The Team

Steve Hinkel

Founder & Principal Consultant

Steve Hinkel is the founder of Leading Edge Consulting and a trusted partner to organizations seeking to understand and transform their culture in a meaningful, human-centered way.

Beginning his career in 2008 as an external consultant, Steve spent a decade honing his expertise in enhancing human systems to boost motivation, alignment, productivity, and workplace culture. In 2018, he transitioned into internal roles, serving in HR, Talent Development, Learning & Development, and Innovation/Design Thinking coaching and consulting. This blend of external and internal experience grants him a unique perspective, understanding both the structural and behavioral drivers of effective organizational change.

Over the past 15+ years, Steve has collaborated with teams at leading organizations such as Facebook, Google, Genentech, Intuit, LinkedIn, Stanford University, and Turner Construction. He specializes in uncovering the cultural patterns that influence organizational operations and guiding teams to reshape these patterns to achieve their objectives. His approach combines experiential facilitation, behavioral observation, and organizational insight, enabling him to conduct culture assessments that not only identify current dynamics but also build momentum for meaningful change.

Steve holds an MBA with an emphasis in Organizational Development and an Advanced Certificate in Global Leadership Development. He integrates formal training with the empathy of a seasoned facilitator and coach, creating environments where individuals at all levels feel safe to reflect, challenge assumptions, and actively participate in designing a more intentional and effective organizational culture.
